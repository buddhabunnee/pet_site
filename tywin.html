Background
Tywin was adopted from Five Acres Animal Shelter in St. Charles, MO. He was transferred from another shelter that felt he was not a good adoption candidate. We aren't sure why this might've been; perhaps it was some ill-perceived issue, since some animals just don't thrive in a shelter environment. Who knows. Either way, he was rescued by my roommates and is no longer a homeless animal! YAY!

Tywin is basically a blonde with blue eyes. So is this guy, Tywin Lannister, a character from A Song of Ice and Fire / Game of Thrones.

Personality
Tywin is a very good boy. He was already housebroken when he came home from the shelter. He is good on the leash, and can even go on walks without one on at all. He is mostly laid back, with an occasional wild streak. He will always perk up at the prospect of play. Like most dogs, he loves going outside and on adventures. He is also still trying to win over the cats, approaching them with typical submissive and playful behavior, basically screaming: "let's be friends!" The problem is, Tywin and the kitties speak completely different languages. He doesn't understand their standoffish/aggressive behavior, and the kitties either don't understand his playful/friendly behavior, or don't care to be friends with a dog.

We are still learning about Tywin, but so far he has been a nearly perfect companion. He has torn up a few things and made a few escapes, but what dog hasn't? Show me a dog who is perfect and I'll show you a liar!
